# SCREENPLAY: SNACK CHEMICAL EXPOSÉ

## 

**TITLE:** "Why Your Snacks Have Chemicals Banned in Europe"

### [0-2s] HOOK
**VISUAL:** Close-up of creator's shocked face holding a bag of Doritos Nacho Cheese, then quick cut to her holding a European equivalent (Walkers Crisps). Split screen showing both packages.

**VOICEOVER:** (Dramatic, concerned tone) "Ever wondered why some snacks in the US contain chemicals banned in Europe?"

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "BANNED IN EUROPE ❌"

**SOUND:** Dramatic sting, then upbeat background music

### [2-10s] SETUP
**VISUAL:** Creator sits at kitchen counter with multiple snack packages spread out - US versions (Doritos, Cheetos, Mountain Dew, Skittles) on left, European versions on right. She picks up magnifying glass.

**VOICEOVER:** (Educational, slightly outraged) "Let me show you something that'll blow your mind. These are the EXACT same brands, but look at what's hiding in the American versions."

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "SAME BRANDS, DIFFERENT INGREDIENTS"

### [10-25s] CORE PART 1
**VISUAL:** Extreme close-up of Doritos ingredient list through magnifying glass, finger pointing to specific additives. Cut to European Walkers equivalent showing cleaner ingredient list.

**VOICEOVER:** (Detailed, investigative tone) "US Doritos contain Yellow 6, Yellow 5, and Red 40 - petroleum-based dyes linked to hyperactivity in children. The European Union banned these in 2010 after studies showed behavioral issues. But Frito-Lay uses paprika and natural colors in their European Doritos instead."

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "YELLOW 6, YELLOW 5, RED 40" → "BANNED EU 2010"

### [25-40s] CORE PART 2
**VISUAL:** Creator holds up Mountain Dew, then European equivalent. Quick montage showing other examples - Skittles, Cheetos. Animation graphic showing US map vs EU map with banned symbols.

**VOICEOVER:** (Building intensity) "Mountain Dew uses Brominated Vegetable Oil - banned in Europe since 2020 because it can cause organ damage. And here's the kicker: since 1996, the FDA's GRAS self-affirmation process lets companies add new chemicals to our food WITHOUT notifying the government first. Meanwhile, US adult obesity has skyrocketed from 13.4% in 1960 to 43% in 2024."

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "BVO - ORGAN DAMAGE" / "1996: COMPANIES SELF-REGULATE" / "OBESITY: 13.4% → 43%"

### [40-45s] CTA
**VISUAL:** Creator looking directly at camera, holding both versions of snacks, then tossing US version aside and taking a bite of homemade veggie chips.

**VOICEOVER:** (Empowering, direct) "Your health shouldn't depend on your zip code. Read those labels, gorgeous!"

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "READ YOUR LABELS ✨"

**SOUND:** Music crescendo and fade
