## 

**TITLE:** Egg Truth: What Color Says About Your Health!

**DURATION:** 45 seconds

---

### [0-2s] HOOK
**VISUAL:** Extreme close-up of creator's face, eyebrow raised playfully, holding two different egg cartons at eye level
**VOICEOVER:** "Can you guess which egg is healthier just by looking?" *(teasing, confident tone)*
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "EGG MYSTERY 🥚"
**SOUND/MUSIC:** Upbeat, mysterious intro beat

---

### [2-10s] SETUP
**VISUAL:** Medium shot showing creator placing two egg cartons on marble countertop. Left carton clearly labeled "Organic Pasture-Raised" ($6-7 price tag visible), right carton "Conventional Cage-Free" ($3 price tag visible). Camera zooms in on labels.
**VOICEOVER:** "These Vital Farms pasture-raised eggs cost twice as much as these Eggland's Best conventional ones. But here's what that extra money actually buys you..." *(building anticipation)*
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "PASTURE-RAISED vs CONVENTIONAL"
**SOUND/MUSIC:** Building tension, slight pause

---

### [10-35s] CORE
**VISUAL:** 
- Close-up hands cracking first conventional egg into clear glass bowl (left side)
- Immediately crack pasture-raised egg into second bowl (right side)
- Extreme close-up comparison shot of both yolks side by side - pale yellow vs deep orange
- Quick cut to animated graphics showing nutritional differences

**VOICEOVER:** "Look at this difference! The conventional yolk is pale yellow because those hens eat processed soy and corn feed in confined spaces. But this deep orange yolk? That's from hens eating grass, insects, and plants outdoors. According to a 2019 study from Penn State University, pasture-raised eggs have two times more omega-3 fatty acids, three times more vitamin D, and six times more vitamin E than conventional eggs. That orange color? That's beta-carotene - the same antioxidant that makes carrots orange!" *(excited, educational tone)*

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** 
- "PALE = PROCESSED FEED"
- "ORANGE = NATURAL DIET"
- "2x OMEGA-3s | 3x VITAMIN D | 6x VITAMIN E"

---

### [35-45s] CTA
**VISUAL:** Creator whisking both eggs in pan, the color difference still visible. Final shot of her taking a bite of the scrambled pasture-raised eggs with an satisfied expression.
**VOICEOVER:** "Your body deserves the real deal, not factory farm eggs. Next time you're at the store, look for that deep orange yolk - your heart and brain will thank you! What's your favorite way to eat eggs?" *(encouraging, warm tone)*
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "CHOOSE ORANGE YOLKS ✨"
**SOUND/MUSIC:** Satisfying sizzle sounds, upbeat outro

### PRODUCTION DETAILS

**PROPS NEEDED:**
- 1 carton Vital Farms Pasture-Raised Organic eggs
- 1 carton Eggland's Best Conventional eggs  
- 2 clear glass bowls
- Non-stick pan
- Whisk
- Marble countertop or white cutting board
- Price tags ($6.99 and $2.99)

**SHOOTING NOTES:**
- Use macro lens for extreme yolk close-ups
- Ensure good lighting to capture color difference
- Shoot at 60fps for slow-motion crack sequences
- Keep bowls identical for fair comparison
- Use white/neutral background to make colors pop
