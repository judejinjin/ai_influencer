**TITLE:** The Secret Additive in Your Pork!
**DURATION:** 45 seconds
**FORMAT:** Vertical (9:16) TikTok

**PROPS NEEDED:**
- Raw pork chop or tenderloin
- Printed world map
- Smartphone for heart rate monitor effect
- Good lighting setup
- Ring light or softbox

**SHOOTING NOTES:**
- Use macro lens or phone's portrait mode for extreme close-ups
- Ensure good lighting on pork to show texture clearly
- Film map sequence with overhead shot
- Use red/warning color grading for dramatic effect

**[0-2s] HOOK**
**VISUAL:** Extreme close-up of raw pork with dramatic red lighting. Large question mark overlay pulsing on screen.
**VOICEOVER:** (Urgent, concerned tone) "Did you know your pork might contain a banned additive?"
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "BANNED ADDITIVE IN YOUR PORK?!" (bold red text)
**SOUND:** Dramatic sting sound effect

**[2-10s] SETUP**
**VISUAL:** Cut to creator on camera, holding the raw pork. Medium shot, kitchen background.
**VOICEOVER:** (Conversational but serious) "I'm talking about ractopamine - a growth promoter pumped into pigs, cattle, and turkeys here in the US."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "RACTOPAMINE = GROWTH PROMOTER"
**SOUND:** Subtle tension music begins

**[10-30s] CORE - PART 1**
**VISUAL:** Overhead shot of world map. Creator's hands pointing to different countries, with red X's appearing over banned regions.
**VOICEOVER:** (Educational tone) "This drug is banned in over 160 countries including the entire European Union, China, and Russia. Why? Because ractopamine residues in meat can mess with your cardiovascular system."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "BANNED IN 160+ COUNTRIES" then "CARDIOVASCULAR RISKS"
**SOUND:** Heartbeat sound effect building

**[30-42s] CORE - PART 2**
**VISUAL:** Close-up of creator's face, then cut to phone showing heart rate monitor app with erratic readings.
**VOICEOVER:** (Concerned tone) "We're talking increased heart rate, potential cardiac issues. Meanwhile, US obesity rates have skyrocketed from 13.4% in 1960 to 43% in 2024. Coincidence?"
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "1960: 13.4% OBESE" → "2024: 43% OBESE"
**SOUND:** Heart monitor beeping intensifies

**[42-45s] CTA**
**VISUAL:** Creator looking directly at camera, holding up the pork.
**VOICEOVER:** (Empowering tone) "Choose organic, ractopamine-free meat. Your heart will thank you."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "CHOOSE ORGANIC 💚"
**SOUND:** Music resolves to hopeful tone
