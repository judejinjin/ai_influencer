**TITLE:** Battle of the Broths: Homemade vs. Bouillon Cubes

### [0-2s] HOOK
**VISUAL:** Close-up of creator's face, sultry smile, holding a steaming bowl of golden broth in one hand and a bouillon cube in the other. Camera slowly pulls back to reveal kitchen counter setup.

**VOICEOVER:** "Which broth is better for your health? Let's find out!" (confident, teasing tone)

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "BROTH BATTLE 🥊"

**SOUND/MUSIC:** Upbeat, suspenseful intro beat

**VISUAL:** Split-screen setup - left side shows homemade golden chicken broth in clear glass bowl, right side shows Knorr chicken bouillon cube next to empty bowl. Creator's hands gesture between both sides.

**VOICEOVER:** "On this side, we have homemade chicken broth made with organic chicken, carrots, celery, onions, and fresh herbs." (gestures left) "And over here, the popular Knorr chicken bouillon cube that millions use daily." (gestures right)

**VOICEOVER:** "Here's what shocked me - a single Knorr bouillon cube contains 900 milligrams of sodium. That's nearly half your daily limit according to FDA guidelines! Plus monosodium glutamate, hydrolyzed corn protein, and artificial chicken flavor."

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "1 Knorr cube = 900mg sodium" "Daily limit = 2,300mg"

**VISUAL:** Side-by-side comparison shots. Creator tastes homemade broth, closes eyes in satisfaction. Then tastes bouillon broth, makes subtle "meh" face.

**VOICEOVER:** "My homemade version? Just 40 milligrams of natural sodium from the chicken and vegetables. No MSG, no artificial flavors, no preservatives. Just pure, nourishing ingredients your body actually recognizes."

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "Homemade = 40mg sodium" "REAL ingredients only ✅"

### [43-45s] CTA
**VISUAL:** Creator leans toward camera, holding bowl of homemade broth, steam rising. Winks playfully.

**VOICEOVER:** "Your body will thank you for choosing real food, mi amor!"

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "Which team are you on? 👇"
