# SCREENPLAY: AMERICA'S GROWING PROBLEM



**TITLE:** America's Growing Problem: Obesity & Deregulation

**PROPS NEEDED:**
- Printed graph showing obesity rates 1960-2024
- Two identical white plates
- Healthy meal: grilled chicken, quinoa, steamed broccoli
- Processed snacks: Hostess Twinkie, Dunkin' Donuts coffee creamer, Skittles
- Ring light for even lighting
- Phone tripod
- Kitchen counter/clean background

**[0-2s] HOOK**
- **VISUAL:** Extreme close-up of creator's face, serious expression, holding up obesity graph
- **VOICEOVER:** (Urgently) "Why has American obesity SKYROCKETED?" 
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "43% of US adults are obese in 2024"
- **SOUND:** Sharp attention-grabbing sound effect

**[2-10s] SETUP**
- **VISUAL:** Medium shot, creator places graph on counter, gestures to camera
- **VOICEOVER:** (Concerned tone) "In 1960, only 13.4% of American adults were obese. By 2024? We hit 43%. That's TRIPLE in just 64 years."
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** Split screen showing "1960: 13.4%" vs "2024: 43.0%"
- **SOUND:** Subtle dramatic music building

**[10-45s] CORE**
- **VISUAL:** [10-15s] Two plates side by side - healthy meal vs processed foods
- **VOICEOVER:** "Here's what other countries figured out that we didn't. Take titanium dioxide - it's in your Skittles, Dunkin' coffee creamer, and Hostess Twinkies."
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "Titanium Dioxide (E171) - In your food!"

- **VISUAL:** [15-25s] Close-up of Skittles package, then coffee creamer, then Twinkie
- **VOICEOVER:** "The European Union banned this additive in 2022 after their food safety authority found it could damage your DNA. But here in America? Still totally legal."
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "BANNED in EU 2022" / "LEGAL in USA"

- **VISUAL:** [25-35s] Creator picks up graph, points to steep incline after 1980s
- **VOICEOVER:** "Notice how our obesity rates started climbing fastest in the 1980s and 90s? That's exactly when food deregulation kicked into high gear."
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "1980: 15.0%" → "2000: 30.5%" → "2024: 43.0%"

- **VISUAL:** [35-45s] Split screen: European grocery aisle vs American processed food aisle
- **VOICEOVER:** "While Europe was tightening food safety laws, America was loosening them. Kids went from 5% obesity in 1960 to 21% today."
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "Children's obesity: 1960: 5.0% → 2024: 21.0%"

**[45-60s] CTA**
- **VISUAL:** Creator leans into camera, passionate expression
- **VOICEOVER:** (Emphatically) "We deserve the same food safety standards as Europe. Start reading labels, demand better, and let's make our voices heard!"
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "Read your labels! 👀"
- **VISUAL:** [55-60s] Creator holds up both plates again
- **VOICEOVER:** "Which plate are you choosing today?"
- **SOUND:** Music crescendo and fade

**SHOOTING NOTES:**
- Use ring light for consistent lighting on face and food
- Shoot graph segments with slight downward angle to avoid glare
- Keep processed foods in frame long enough for viewers to read labels
- Use quick cuts during statistics to maintain energy

**HASHTAGS:** #FoodSafety #ObesityCrisis #FoodRegulation #HealthyEating #ProcessedFoods #FoodAdditive #HealthAwareness #FoodPolicy

**MUSIC:** Trending sound with dramatic buildup, or original audio with subtle tension-building background music
