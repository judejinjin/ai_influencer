# SCREENPLAY: THE US OBESITY EPIDEMIC & DEREGULATION



## PROPS NEEDED:
- Large printed graph showing US obesity rates 1960-2024
- Red marker/highlighter
- Ring light for even lighting
- Tripod for steady shots
- Various cereal boxes (for BHT example)
- White backdrop or clean kitchen counter

## SHOOTING NOTES:
- Use portrait orientation (9:16) for TikTok
- Maintain eye-level camera angle for connection
- Ensure good lighting on face and graph
- Keep movements smooth and deliberate
- Film multiple takes of graph pointing for editing options

---

### [0-2s] HOOK
**VISUAL:** Close-up of creator's face, serious expression, holding up the obesity graph
**VOICEOVER:** "US obesity rates have skyrocketed from 13% to 43% in just 60 years..." (concerned tone)
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "13% → 43% in 60 years"
**SOUND:** Dramatic pause music, building tension

### [2-10s] SETUP  
**VISUAL:** Medium shot showing creator pointing to different points on the graph timeline
**VOICEOVER:** "But here's what they don't want you to know — this isn't just about personal choices. It's about what happened to our food system." (leaning in, conspiratorial tone)
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "1960: 13.4% adults obese" → "2024: 43% adults obese"
**SOUND:** Subtle investigative music

### [10-45s] CORE
**VISUAL:** Split between creator explaining and close-ups of cereal boxes, then back to graph
**VOICEOVER:** "While Europe banned additives like BHT — found in Lucky Charms, Frosted Flakes, and Rice Krispies — the US kept them legal. BHT is linked to liver damage and hormone disruption in studies. Look at this timeline..." (pointing to graph) "1980s deregulation under Reagan coincides with obesity jumping from 15% to over 40% today. CDC data shows children's obesity tripled from 5% in 1960 to 21% by 2024."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "BHT: BANNED in UK/EU" → "LEGAL in US cereals" → "Obesity rates TRIPLED in kids"
**SOUND:** Educational background music, slightly urgent

### [45-60s] CTA
**VISUAL:** Creator looking directly at camera, graph visible in background
**VOICEOVER:** "The EU protects their people — why doesn't the US protect us? Start reading labels, choose whole foods, and demand better. What do you think — coincidence or correlation?" (challenging tone)
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "Read your labels!" → "Follow for more food truths"
**SOUND:** Empowering, call-to-action music
