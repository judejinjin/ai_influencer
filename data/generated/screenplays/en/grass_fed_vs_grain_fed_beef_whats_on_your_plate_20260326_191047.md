# SCREENPLAY: GRASS-FED VS. GRAIN-FED BEEF



**TITLE:** "Grass-Fed vs. Grain-Fed Beef: What's on Your Plate?"

## SCREENPLAY

### [0-2s] HOOK
**VISUAL:** Extreme close-up split screen of two raw steaks side by side on white cutting board. Left steak is deep burgundy red, right steak is pale pink-red.
**VOICEOVER:** (Confident, slightly teasing) "Can you spot the difference in just 2 seconds?"
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "SPOT THE DIFFERENCE" (bold white text)
**SOUND:** Sharp sizzle sound effect

### [2-10s] SETUP  
**VISUAL:** Medium shot of creator holding both steaks, one in each hand. Camera slowly zooms in on her face, then cuts to overhead shot of both steaks being placed in a hot cast iron pan simultaneously.
**VOICEOVER:** (Educational but sexy tone) "One of these steaks will give you 2 to 5 times more omega-3s than the other. The question is... which one?"
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "2-5x MORE OMEGA-3s" (appears over sizzling pan)
**SOUND:** Pan sizzling intensifies

### [10-35s] CORE
**VISUAL:** Split screen cooking shot - left side shows grass-fed steak with golden-yellow fat rendering, right side shows grain-fed with white fat. Close-up of creator's hands flipping both steaks with spatula.
**VOICEOVER:** (Matter-of-fact, building excitement) "The deep red one? That's 100% grass-fed, grass-finished beef. See that golden fat? That's beta-carotene from grass. The pale one comes from conventional feedlots - corn, soy, antibiotics, and growth hormones."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "GRASS-FED: Golden fat = Beta-carotene" / "GRAIN-FED: White fat = Feedlot diet"

**VISUAL:** Quick montage: Close-up of cooked steaks side by side, creator cutting into both, revealing the interior colors.
**VOICEOVER:** (Passionate, leaning in) "According to nutrition research from 2023, grass-fed beef contains higher levels of conjugated linoleic acid, more antioxidant vitamins A and E, and a healthier omega-6 to omega-3 ratio."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "✓ Higher CLA ✓ More Vitamins A & E ✓ Better Omega Ratio" (checkmarks appear sequentially)

### [35-45s] CTA
**VISUAL:** Creator taking a bite of the grass-fed steak, eyes closed in satisfaction. Camera pulls back to show both plated steaks with clear color difference.
**VOICEOVER:** (Satisfied, direct to camera) "Your body deserves the good stuff. Next time you're at the butcher, ask the right questions."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "ASK YOUR BUTCHER: Grass-fed? Grass-finished?"
**SOUND:** Satisfying bite sound, music builds to crescendo

## PRODUCTION DETAILS

**PROPS NEEDED:**
- 1 grass-fed, grass-finished ribeye or strip steak
- 1 conventional grain-fed ribeye or strip steak (same cut)
- Cast iron skillet or heavy pan
- Spatula
- White cutting board
- Sharp knife
- Two white plates for plating

**SHOOTING NOTES:**
- Use bright, natural lighting to capture true color differences
- Overhead rig essential for split-screen cooking shots
- Macro lens for extreme close-ups of fat color and texture
- Shoot steaks at room temperature for best color contrast
- Have backup steaks in case of overcooking
