### [10-35s] CORE
**VISUAL:** Split screen effect. Left side shows the smoking pan with thermometer reading 400°F+. Right side shows creator holding the olive oil bottle, pointing to it.

**VOICEOVER:** (Educational, passionate) "Extra virgin olive oil contains oleocanthal - a natural anti-inflammatory compound that works similarly to ibuprofen according to research published in Nature journal in 2005. ## PRODUCTION DETAILS

**PROPS NEEDED:**
- 1 bottle Colavita Extra Virgin Olive Oil (or similar quality brand)
- Gas/electric stove
- Stainless steel pan
- Kitchen thermometer
- White ceramic plate
- Grilled vegetables (bell peppers, zucchini, eggplant)
- Small glass bowl for drizzling

**SHOOTING NOTES:**
- Use ring light for face close-ups to catch eye reflections
- Shoot smoking pan sequence with good ventilation
- Use macro lens for oil drizzling shots
- Ensure good contrast between golden oil and colorful vegetables
- Film split-screen elements separately, combine in post
