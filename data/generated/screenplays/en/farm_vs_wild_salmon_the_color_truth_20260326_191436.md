# SALMON COLOR TRUTH SCREENPLAY

## 

**TITLE:** Farm vs. Wild Salmon: The Color Truth  
**DURATION:** 45 seconds  
**FORMAT:** Vertical (9:16) for TikTok/Instagram Reels  

### [0-2s] HOOK
**VISUAL:** Extreme close-up of creator's face, bright kitchen lighting, she's holding two salmon fillets - one vibrant pink, one deeper red-orange
**VOICEOVER:** "Why is your salmon pink? Let's find out!" (playful, intrigued tone)
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "SALMON COLOR MYSTERY 🐟"
**SOUND:** Upbeat, mysterious intro music with a "ding" sound effect

### [2-10s] SETUP  
**VISUAL:** Cut to overhead shot of kitchen counter. Creator places two salmon fillets side by side - one bright pink (farmed), one deeper red-orange (wild). Her manicured hands point to each piece.
**VOICEOVER:** "Same fish, totally different colors. One's from a farm, one's wild-caught sockeye. But here's what the salmon industry doesn't want you to know..." (conspiratorial whisper)
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "FARMED" (pointing to pink) | "WILD SOCKEYE" (pointing to red-orange)
**SOUND:** Dramatic pause music

### [10-35s] CORE
**VISUAL:** Quick montage:
- Close-up of creator holding a printed salmon color chart (like paint swatches)
- Zoom in on the color chart showing numbered pink shades
- Cut back to creator's face, looking shocked
- Side-by-side comparison of the fillets again
- Creator pointing to ingredients list on screen

**VOICEOVER:** "Farm-raised salmon is literally gray without artificial coloring. Salmon farms use this actual color chart - like choosing paint swatches - to pick how pink they want their fish. They add synthetic astaxanthin made from petrochemicals to the feed. Wild sockeye gets its gorgeous color naturally from eating krill and smaller fish. Plus, farmed salmon has higher levels of PCBs and dioxins - chemicals you definitely don't want on your plate."

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** 
- "GRAY WITHOUT DYE" (over farmed salmon)
- "SYNTHETIC ASTAXANTHIN FROM PETROCHEMICALS" 
- "NATURAL COLOR FROM KRILL" (over wild salmon)
- "HIGHER PCBs & DIOXINS IN FARMED"

**SOUND:** Educational background music, slightly dramatic

### [35-45s] CTA
**VISUAL:** Creator back on camera, holding up the wild salmon fillet with a confident smile
**VOICEOVER:** "So next time you're shopping, look for wild-caught sockeye, king, or coho. Your body will thank you! What other 'natural' foods should we investigate next?" (wink)
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "CHOOSE WILD-CAUGHT 🐟"
**SOUND:** Upbeat conclusion music with notification sound

---

### PROPS NEEDED:
- 2 salmon fillets (1 farmed Atlantic, 1 wild sockeye)
- Printed salmon color chart (SalmoFan color chart replica)
- Clean white kitchen counter/cutting board
- Good lighting setup

### SHOOTING NOTES:
- Use macro lens for extreme close-ups of fish texture
- Ensure color accuracy in lighting - no yellow/blue tints
- Shoot overhead shots with phone mounted on tripod
- Keep background clean and minimal
- Film multiple takes of color chart reveal for best reaction
