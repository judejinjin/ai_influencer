### PROPS NEEDED:
- Fresh carrot sticks (6-8 pieces)
- 2 identical mason jars with lids
- Labels: "HOMEMADE RANCH" and "BOTTLED RANCH"
- Homemade ranch dressing (Greek yogurt, dill, chives, garlic powder, onion powder, lemon juice)
- Hidden Valley Ranch Original dressing
- Fresh dill sprigs and chives for background
- Small mixing bowl and whisk (for CTA montage)

### SHOOTING NOTES:
- Use natural lighting or ring light for ingredient close-ups
- Shoot on marble or clean white countertop for contrast
- Keep camera steady for ingredient list overlays
- Film multiple takes of the dipping action for best "plop" sound
- Ensure hands and nails are camera-ready for close-ups
