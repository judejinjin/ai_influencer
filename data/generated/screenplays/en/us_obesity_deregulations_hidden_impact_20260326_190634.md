# SCREENPLAY: US OBESITY & DEREGULATION

## 

**TITLE:** "US Obesity: Deregulation's Hidden Impact"

### [0-2s] HOOK
**VISUAL:** Close-up shot of creator's glossy lips taking a slow, deliberate bite of a glazed donut. Camera pulls back to reveal her holding it with a concerned expression.

**VOICEOVER:** (Sultry but serious tone) "What's really fueling this obesity epidemic?"

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "The shocking truth about US food regulation"

**SOUND/MUSIC:** Dramatic pause with subtle tension-building music

---

### [2-10s] SETUP
**VISUAL:** Creator sets donut down, crosses arms. Cut to animated graph showing US obesity rates climbing from 1960 to 2024. US flag overlay appears semi-transparent.

**VOICEOVER:** (Educational, building urgency) "In 1960, only 13.4% of American adults were obese. By 2024? A staggering 43%. That's not just lifestyle changes, mi amor."

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** 
- "1960: 13.4% obese"
- "2024: 43.0% obese"

**SOUND/MUSIC:** Upbeat but serious educational tone

---

### [10-45s] CORE
**VISUAL:** Split screen: Creator pointing at ingredients list on donut package vs. European equivalent. Cut to empty FDA office desk with papers scattered. Back to creator holding up different processed foods.

**VOICEOVER:** (Passionate, revealing tone) "Here's what they don't want you to know. That donut contains brominated vegetable oil — BVO. The FDA called it 'Generally Recognized as Safe' in 1958. But guess what? The EU and Japan banned it DECADES ago. It took until 2023 — sixty-five years later — for the FDA to even propose removing it."

**VISUAL:** Creator picks up different snack packages, pointing at ingredients.

**VOICEOVER:** (Continued) "Mountain Dew? Has BVO. Some baked goods? BVO. While European kids grew up without these additives, American obesity rates tripled. Children went from 5% obese in 1960 to 21% in 2024."

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:**
- "BVO: GRAS since 1958"
- "EU/Japan: BANNED for decades"
- "Kids: 5% → 21% obese"

---

### [45-60s] CTA
**VISUAL:** Creator leans into camera confidently, tossing the donut in trash behind her. Points directly at viewer.

**VOICEOVER:** (Empowering, direct) "It's time to read those labels, beautiful. Your health can't wait another 65 years for them to act. Follow me for more food truths they don't want you to know."

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "Read your labels 👆"

**SOUND/MUSIC:** Confident, empowering outro music
