# GREEK YOGURT RANCH VS. STORE-BOUGHT: TASTE TEST SCREENPLAY

## 

**PROPS NEEDED:**
- 2 identical white plates
- Mixed greens salad (2 portions)
- Homemade Greek yogurt ranch (in small bowl)
- Hidden Valley Ranch bottle
- Small spoons for drizzling
- Good lighting setup
- Phone tripod

**SHOOTING NOTES:**
- Use natural lighting or ring light for food shots
- Shoot overhead for salad comparison shots
- Close-ups on face for reactions
- Keep background clean and minimal
- Film taste reactions authentically - don't overact

---

### [0-2s] HOOK
**VISUAL:** Close-up overhead shot of two identical salad plates side by side. Left plate gets drizzled with creamy white homemade ranch, right plate gets commercial Hidden Valley Ranch.

**VOICEOVER:** (Playful, intrigued tone) "Same salad, two different ranches..."

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "HOMEMADE vs STORE-BOUGHT"

**SOUND:** Upbeat, curious music starts

---

### [2-10s] SETUP  
**VISUAL:** Medium shot of creator holding a fork, looking directly at camera with a slight smirk. Cut to close-up of her taking a deliberate bite from the left plate (homemade ranch).

**VOICEOVER:** "Let's see if there's actually a difference between my Greek yogurt ranch and this bottle that's been in my fridge for... honestly, too long."

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "TASTE TEST TIME"

**SOUND:** Music continues, slight pause for emphasis

---

### [10-45s] CORE
**VISUAL:** Extreme close-up of her face showing genuine pleasure after tasting homemade version. Cut to her picking up fork for store-bought version. Close-up of her face showing immediate change in expression - not disgusted, but clearly less impressed. Split screen showing both salads. Cut to her holding Hidden Valley bottle, pointing to ingredients label.

**VOICEOVER:** (Enthusiastic) "Okay, WOW. The homemade one is so fresh and creamy!" (Tone shifts to informative) "But here's what's really wild - my version has five ingredients: Greek yogurt, garlic, dill, chives, and a splash of lemon. This Hidden Valley Ranch? Soybean oil, MSG, sodium benzoate, and calcium disodium EDTA - which is literally an industrial chelating agent used in manufacturing." (Slightly sassy) "I don't know about you, but I prefer my salad dressing without industrial chemicals."

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** 
- "HOMEMADE: 5 ingredients"
- "HIDDEN VALLEY: 20+ ingredients"
- "Calcium disodium EDTA = industrial chemical"

**SOUND:** Music builds slightly during the reveal

---

### [45-50s] CTA
**VISUAL:** Back to overhead shot of both plates, then close-up of creator pointing to homemade version with fork.

**VOICEOVER:** (Direct, encouraging tone) "Trust me, make your own ranch. Your taste buds and your body will thank you. Recipe's in my bio!"

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "RECIPE IN BIO ⬇️"

**SOUND:** Music crescendos and fades
