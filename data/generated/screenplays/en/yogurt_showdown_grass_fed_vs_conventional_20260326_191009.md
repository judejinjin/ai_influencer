# YOGURT SHOWDOWN SCREENPLAY

## 

### **HOOK [0-2s]**
**VISUAL:** Extreme close-up of two identical white spoons hovering over two different yogurt containers. Camera slowly pulls back to reveal the containers side by side on a marble countertop. Creator's manicured hands come into frame.

**VOICEOVER:** "Can you spot the healthier yogurt?" (teasing, confident tone)

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "Which one? 🤔"

**SOUND/MUSIC:** Suspenseful buildup sound effect

### **SETUP [2-10s]**
**VISUAL:** Medium shot of creator holding up two yogurt containers - Maple Hill Creamery 100% Grass-Fed Whole Milk Yogurt (left) and Dannon Two Good Greek Yogurt (right). She places them on counter, then opens both lids with dramatic flair.

**VOICEOVER:** "This is Maple Hill's grass-fed whole milk yogurt versus Dannon's conventional Greek yogurt. They look similar, but here's what most people don't know..." (educational, building intrigue)

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** Split screen labels: "GRASS-FED" | "CONVENTIONAL"

**SOUND/MUSIC:** Light, upbeat background music begins

### **CORE [10-40s]**
**VISUAL:** Close-up shot of both opened containers. The grass-fed yogurt shows a visible cream layer on top, while the conventional yogurt appears uniform. Creator dips spoon into grass-fed yogurt first, showing the creamy texture clinging to spoon.

**VOICEOVER:** "See this cream layer? That's because Maple Hill doesn't homogenize their milk - the fat globules stay naturally large, just like nature intended." (pointing with spoon)

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "Non-homogenized = Natural fat structure"

**VISUAL:** Cut to dipping spoon into conventional yogurt - much thinner consistency. Creator holds both spoons side by side for comparison.

**VOICEOVER:** "Conventional yogurts like Dannon use homogenized milk where fat particles are mechanically broken down into tiny pieces. According to dairy science research from 2022, these micro-particles may bypass normal digestion differently than natural fat globules."

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "Homogenized = Broken fat particles"

**VISUAL:** Creator takes a taste of each yogurt, making thoughtful expressions. Camera zooms in on her face showing genuine reaction.

**VOICEOVER:** "Plus, grass-fed dairy contains up to 5 times more omega-3 fatty acids and double the CLA - that's conjugated linoleic acid - compared to grain-fed cows. The USDA confirmed this in their 2023 nutritional analysis."

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "5x more omega-3s! 🌱"

### **CTA [40-45s]**
**VISUAL:** Creator leans into camera with both yogurt containers, holding the grass-fed one slightly forward.

**VOICEOVER:** "Your body deserves the real deal. Which yogurt are you choosing?" (direct, empowering tone)

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "Team Grass-Fed? Comment 🌱"

**SOUND/MUSIC:** Music builds to satisfying conclusion

---

### **PROPS NEEDED:**
- Maple Hill Creamery 100% Grass-Fed Whole Milk Yogurt
- Dannon Two Good Greek Yogurt (or similar conventional brand)
- 2 identical silver spoons
- Marble or clean white countertop surface

### **SHOOTING NOTES:**
- Use natural lighting or ring light for clean, bright appearance
- Shoot at 60fps for smooth spoon movements
- Keep camera steady during close-ups of yogurt textures
- Ensure cream layer is visible in grass-fed yogurt before filming
