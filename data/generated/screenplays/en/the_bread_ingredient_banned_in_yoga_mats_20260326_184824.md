# SCREENPLAY: THE BREAD INGREDIENT BANNED IN YOGA MATS

## 

### [0-2s] HOOK
**VISUAL:** Extreme close-up of creator's shocked face, eyes wide, holding a slice of Wonder Bread in one hand and a purple yoga mat in the other
**VOICEOVER:** (Dramatic whisper) "Girl, your bread shares an ingredient with your yoga mat!"
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "SAME INGREDIENT?!" (bold red text with shock emoji)
**SOUND/MUSIC:** Record scratch sound effect, then upbeat investigative music

### [2-10s] SETUP
**VISUAL:** Split screen - left side shows Wonder Bread package being flipped to show ingredients label, right side shows yoga mat close-up texture
**VOICEOVER:** (Conversational, slightly incredulous) "I'm not even kidding. That fluffy white bread you're eating? It contains azodicarbonamide - the same chemical used to make yoga mats bouncy and shoe soles flexible."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "AZODICARBONAMIDE" appears with arrow pointing to both items
**SOUND/MUSIC:** Investigative music continues

### [10-45s] CORE
**VISUAL:** [10-15s] Creator pointing at Wonder Bread ingredients list with manicured finger
**VOICEOVER:** "Wonder Bread, Subway's bread, Jimmy John's - they all use azodicarbonamide as a dough conditioner to make bread fluffier and whiter."

**VISUAL:** [15-25s] Animated world map highlighting EU countries, Australia, and Singapore in red
**VOICEOVER:** "But here's the tea - this chemical is completely banned in the European Union, Australia, and Singapore because when it bakes, it breaks down into semicarbazide, which is a possible carcinogen according to health authorities."

**VISUAL:** [25-35s] Creator holding up homemade bread next to store-bought bread
**VOICEOVER:** "Meanwhile, US obesity rates have skyrocketed from 13.4% of adults in 1960 to 43% in 2024. Coincidence? I think not, babe."

**VISUAL:** [35-45s] Close-up of creator's hands tearing apart both breads, showing texture differences
**VOICEOVER:** "The EU requires companies to use safer alternatives like ascorbic acid - vitamin C - to achieve the same effect."

### [45-60s] CTA
**VISUAL:** Creator taking a bite of homemade bread with satisfied expression, then pointing directly at camera
**VOICEOVER:** (Empowering tone) "So next time you're bread shopping, flip that package and read those ingredients, gorgeous. Your body deserves better than yoga mat chemicals!"
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "READ YOUR LABELS ✨"
**SOUND/MUSIC:** Music builds to energetic finish

---

**PROPS NEEDED:**
- Wonder Bread loaf with visible ingredients label
- Purple/colorful yoga mat
- Homemade bread loaf
- Jimmy John's or Subway bread (if available)

**SHOOTING NOTES:**
- Use macro lens for extreme close-ups of ingredients labels
- Ensure good lighting for reading small text on packages
- Split screen effect can be achieved in post or with two cameras
- Animated map will need motion graphics in post-production
