# SCREENPLAY: THE TRUTH ABOUT YOUR NON-STICK PAN

## 

**[0-2s] HOOK**
- **VISUAL:** Extreme close-up of a heavily scratched black non-stick pan surface, camera slowly zooming in on the deepest scratches. Dramatic lighting creates shadows in the grooves.
- **VOICEOVER:** "Watch this scratched pan closely!" (urgent, concerned tone)
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "WATCH CLOSELY" in bold white letters
- **SOUND/MUSIC:** Suspenseful buildup music, subtle scratching sound effect

**[2-10s] SETUP**
- **VISUAL:** Pull back to show full scratched non-stick pan next to a pristine cast iron skillet. Camera pans between them. Cut to pet bird toy in background "flying away" (moved by hand off-screen).
- **VOICEOVER:** "See those scratches? That's Teflon coating breaking down into your food. Those are PFAS — forever chemicals that never leave your body." (educational, slightly alarmed tone)
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "PFAS = FOREVER CHEMICALS" with red warning symbol
- **SOUND/MUSIC:** Transition to informative background music

**[10-35s] CORE**
- **VISUAL:** Split screen showing the same raw egg being cracked into both pans simultaneously. Close-up of egg cooking in scratched non-stick (looks normal but ominous). Cut to egg in cast iron (sizzling beautifully). Show thermometer reading 500°F over non-stick pan.
- **VOICEOVER:** "When Teflon heats above 500 degrees Fahrenheit, it releases toxic fumes so dangerous they can literally kill pet birds — that's why canaries were used in coal mines as early warning systems. Humans get polymer fume fever from these same fumes. The EPA has linked PFAS exposure to cancer, liver damage, and hormone disruption." (serious, informative tone)
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "500°F = TOXIC FUMES" then "PFAS LINKED TO: Cancer, Liver Damage, Hormone Issues - EPA"
- **SOUND/MUSIC:** Cooking sizzle sounds, maintaining educational music

**[35-45s] CTA**
- **VISUAL:** Side-by-side of finished eggs. Cast iron egg looks golden and perfect. Close-up of creator holding cast iron pan, smiling confidently. Quick montage of stainless steel and ceramic cookware.
- **VOICEOVER:** "Cast iron actually adds beneficial iron to your food and lasts generations. Switch to cast iron, stainless steel, or ceramic-coated cookware. Your body will thank you!" (encouraging, empowering tone)
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "SWITCH TO: Cast Iron, Stainless Steel, Ceramic"
- **SOUND/MUSIC:** Upbeat, empowering music crescendo
