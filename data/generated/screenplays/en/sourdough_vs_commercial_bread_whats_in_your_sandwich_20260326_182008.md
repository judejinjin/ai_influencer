# SCREENPLAY: SOURDOUGH VS. COMMERCIAL BREAD

## 

**TITLE:** Sourdough vs. Commercial Bread: What's in Your Sandwich?

### TIME-CODED SCREENPLAY

**[0-2s] HOOK**
- **VISUAL:** Extreme close-up of creator's face, eyes wide with shock, holding a slice of white Wonder Bread
- **VOICEOVER:** (gasping) "Girl, did you know your bread could contain yoga mat chemicals?" 
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "YOGA MAT CHEMICALS?! 😱"
- **SOUND:** Record scratch sound effect

**[2-10s] SETUP**
- **VISUAL:** Quick montage - creator dramatically drops Wonder Bread, picks up artisanal sourdough loaf, side-by-side on marble countertop
- **VOICEOVER:** (conversational, concerned) "I'm talking about Wonder Bread versus real sourdough. One has four ingredients, the other? Honey, we need to talk."
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "WONDER BREAD vs. SOURDOUGH"
- **SOUND:** Upbeat, slightly dramatic music begins

**[10-25s] CORE PART 1**
- **VISUAL:** Hands tearing open fresh sourdough, steam rising, then close-up of simple ingredients laid out: flour bag, water glass, salt, starter jar
- **VOICEOVER:** (warm, educational) "Real sourdough bread? Four ingredients: flour, water, salt, and wild yeast starter. That's it, baby. Simple, clean, fermented for better digestion."
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "SOURDOUGH: 4 INGREDIENTS ✨"
- **SOUND:** Soft, wholesome acoustic music

**[25-40s] CORE PART 2**
- **VISUAL:** Dramatic zoom on Wonder Bread ingredients label, finger pointing at "azodicarbonamide," then quick cut to yoga mat with text overlay
- **VOICEOVER:** (shocked tone) "Wonder Bread? Over 20 ingredients including azodicarbonamide - the same chemical used to make yoga mats bouncy! The EU banned this stuff in food back in 2005, but the FDA? Still allows up to 45 parts per million in our bread."
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "AZODICARBONAMIDE = YOGA MAT CHEMICAL" / "BANNED IN EU SINCE 2005"
- **SOUND:** Dramatic sting, then informative background music

**[40-45s] CTA**
- **VISUAL:** Creator holding both breads, tossing Wonder Bread aside, taking a big bite of sourdough with satisfaction
- **VOICEOVER:** (empowering) "Your body deserves real food, not lab experiments. Choose sourdough, choose health!"
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "CHOOSE REAL FOOD 💪"
- **SOUND:** Uplifting, confident music crescendo

### PRODUCTION DETAILS

**PROPS NEEDED:**
- 1 loaf Wonder Bread (or similar commercial white bread)
- 1 loaf artisanal whole wheat sourdough
- Flour bag (all-purpose or whole wheat)
- Clear glass of water
- Small bowl of sea salt
- Mason jar with sourdough starter
- 1 yoga mat (any color)
- Marble or wood cutting board/countertop

**SHOOTING NOTES:**
- Use macro lens for extreme close-ups of ingredients and bread texture
- Ensure good lighting on ingredient labels for readability
- Film yoga mat segment separately with good contrast for text overlay
- Capture steam from fresh bread for visual appeal
- Use shallow depth of field for dramatic ingredient reveals
