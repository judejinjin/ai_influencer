### PRODUCTION DETAILS

**PROPS NEEDED:**
- Large stainless steel pot
- 3-4 lbs grass-fed beef bones (knuckle and marrow bones)
- Bragg's Apple Cider Vinegar bottle
- Fresh thyme sprigs
- Bay leaves
- Pink Himalayan salt
- Elegant ceramic mug
- Marble cutting board/counter space

**SHOOTING NOTES:**
- Use ring light for even facial lighting in opening shot
- Overhead rig essential for time-lapse pot shots
- Macro lens for extreme close-ups of bubbling broth
- Shoot time-lapse at 1 frame per 30 seconds for 24-hour cook
- Steam shots work best with back-lighting
- Keep kitchen background minimal and clean
