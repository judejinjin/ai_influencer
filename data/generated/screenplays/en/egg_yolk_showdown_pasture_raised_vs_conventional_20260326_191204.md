### PRODUCTION DETAILS

**PROPS NEEDED:**
- 2 dozen organic pasture-raised eggs (backup needed)
- 1 dozen conventional eggs (Eggland's Best or similar)
- 2 identical clear glass bowls
- Non-stick frying pan
- Spatula
- Paper towels for cleanup
- Price tags or receipts for reference

**SHOOTING NOTES:**
- Use natural window light + ring light for optimal yolk color contrast
- Shoot at 60fps for slow-motion egg crack reveal
- Keep camera steady during side-by-side comparison
- Have backup eggs ready - may need multiple takes for perfect crack
