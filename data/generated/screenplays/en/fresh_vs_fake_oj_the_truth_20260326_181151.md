### PRODUCTION DETAILS

**PROPS NEEDED:**
- 6-8 fresh navel oranges
- Tropicana Pure Premium carton (or similar major brand)
- Manual citrus juicer
- Sharp knife
- Cutting board
- Two clear glasses
- Paper towels

**SHOOTING NOTES:**
- Use ring light or window lighting for even illumination
- Split screen requires two camera angles or post-production editing
- Keep carton ingredient list clearly readable in close-ups
- Ensure fresh juice looks vibrant and appetizing
- Maintain eye contact with camera during key educational moments
