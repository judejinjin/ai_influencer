# SCREENPLAY: OMEGA-3S IN STEAK

## 

**[0-2s] HOOK**
- **VISUAL:** Extreme close-up of creator's face, eyebrow raised with a mischievous smile. Camera pulls back to reveal her holding a bottle of omega-3 supplements in one hand and a beautiful grass-fed steak in the other.
- **VOICEOVER:** "Ever wondered how omega-3s sneak into your beef?" (playful, conspiratorial tone)
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "OMEGA-3s IN STEAK?! 🥩"
- **SOUND/MUSIC:** Upbeat, mysterious intro music with a "ding" sound effect

**[2-10s] SETUP**
- **VISUAL:** Creator dramatically pours omega-3 capsules from the bottle onto the grass-fed steak. Cut to side-by-side shot: grass-fed steak (deep red with yellow fat) vs. grain-fed steak (pale red with white fat).
- **VOICEOVER:** "Plot twist — your steak might be doing your fish oil's job! But only if you choose the right one." (excited, educational tone)
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "GRASS-FED vs GRAIN-FED"
- **SOUND/MUSIC:** Transition to educational background music

**[10-30s] CORE PART 1**
- **VISUAL:** Animated sequence showing a cow grazing on green grass, then omega-3 molecules traveling from the grass into the cow. Cut to creator pointing at the yellow fat on grass-fed steak.
- **VOICEOVER:** "Here's the tea: grass-fed beef contains 2 to 5 times more omega-3 fatty acids than conventional grain-fed beef. According to a 2019 study in the Journal of Animal Science, grass-fed also has higher levels of CLA — that's conjugated linoleic acid — plus more antioxidant vitamins A and E." (informative, enthusiastic)
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "2-5X MORE OMEGA-3s" / "Higher CLA & Vitamins A, E"
- **SOUND/MUSIC:** Continued educational music

**[30-42s] CORE PART 2**
- **VISUAL:** Creator holds up both steaks to camera. Points to the deep red color and yellow fat of grass-fed, then the pale color and white fat of grain-fed. Quick graphic showing omega-6 to omega-3 ratios.
- **VOICEOVER:** "See this yellow fat? That's beta-carotene from grass — nature's multivitamin! Grain-fed cattle get corn and soy, creating an inflammatory omega-6 to omega-3 ratio of 20:1. Grass-fed? A much healthier 3:1 ratio." (confident, teaching tone)
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "YELLOW FAT = BETA-CAROTENE" / "20:1 vs 3:1 RATIO"
- **SOUND/MUSIC:** Music builds slightly

**[42-45s] CTA**
- **VISUAL:** Creator takes a bite of the grass-fed steak, eyes rolling back in pleasure. Winks at camera.
- **VOICEOVER:** "Your heart, brain, and taste buds will thank you. What's your favorite way to cook grass-fed?" (satisfied, inviting tone)
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "COMMENT YOUR FAV RECIPE! 👇"
- **SOUND/MUSIC:** Upbeat outro music

---

**PROPS NEEDED:**
- 1 grass-fed steak (with visible yellow fat)
- 1 grain-fed steak (for comparison)
- 1 bottle of omega-3 supplements
- Small bowl for capsules
- Clean kitchen towels
- Good lighting setup

**SHOOTING NOTES:**
- Use macro lens for extreme close-ups of meat texture and fat color
- Ensure strong lighting to show color differences clearly
- Film animated sequence separately or use motion graphics in post
- Keep steaks cold until shooting to maintain color contrast
- Use white background for steak comparison shots
