# JALAPEÑO METABOLISM BOOST SCREENPLAY

## 

### PROPS NEEDED:
- 3-4 fresh jalapeños (varying sizes)
- Sharp knife
- Wooden cutting board
- Cast iron or stainless steel pan
- Cooking oil (avocado or olive)
- Small bowl for sliced jalapeños
- Kitchen towel
- Ring light or natural lighting setup

### SHOOTING NOTES:
- Use macro lens or close-up setting for jalapeño details
- Shoot at 60fps for smooth slow-motion slicing
- Ensure good lighting on cutting board for white rib visibility
- Have ventilation ready - capsaicin can irritate eyes during filming
- Film multiple takes of sizzling pan for best audio
