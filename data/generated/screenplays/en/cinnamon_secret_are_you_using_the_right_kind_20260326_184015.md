- Bowl of prepared oatmeal
- Printed comparison chart with coumarin levels
- White surface/backdrop for product shots

- Use macro lens for extreme close-ups of cinnamon textures
- Ensure good lighting for powder details - avoid shadows
- Film comparison shots from directly above for clarity
- Use shallow depth of field for face shots to create intimacy
- Capture slow-motion footage of cinnamon sprinkling for visual appeal
