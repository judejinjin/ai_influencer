# BVO: THE 65-YEAR DELAY IN FOOD SAFETY

## 

**DURATION:** 45 seconds

### PROPS NEEDED:
- Mountain Dew or Fanta Orange bottle (brands that contain BVO)
- EU flag printout or graphic
- Japan flag printout or graphic
- Timeline graphic (1958-2023)
- Ring light for dramatic lighting

### SHOOTING NOTES:
- Start with dramatic close-up on bottle label
- Use ring light to create halo effect around creator
- Quick cuts between creator and graphics for dynamic pacing
- End with confident, direct-to-camera delivery

---

### [0-2s] HOOK
**VISUAL:** Extreme close-up of creator's manicured hand holding a Mountain Dew bottle, dramatic lighting highlighting the ingredient label. Quick zoom out to show creator's face with raised eyebrow.

**VOICEOVER:** (Shocked, urgent tone) "This ingredient is banned in Europe!"

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "BANNED IN EUROPE ❌"

**SOUND:** Dramatic sting sound effect

---

### [2-10s] SETUP
**VISUAL:** Creator holds bottle at eye level, pointing to ingredient list. Cut to close-up of label showing "brominated vegetable oil."

**VOICEOVER:** (Educational, slightly incredulous) "Brominated vegetable oil — or BVO — is in Mountain Dew, Fanta Orange, and other citrus sodas. The EU and Japan banned this stuff decades ago, but guess what?"

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "BVO = Brominated Vegetable Oil"

**SOUND:** Upbeat but tense background music

---

### [10-35s] CORE
**VISUAL:** Split screen showing EU and Japan flags. Cut to timeline graphic showing 1958-2023. Creator gestures expressively while speaking. Quick flash of obesity statistics: "1960: 13.4%" morphing to "2024: 43.0%"

**VOICEOVER:** (Building intensity) "The FDA gave BVO 'Generally Recognized as Safe' status in 1958 — that's 65 years ago! While Europe and Japan said 'no way' and banned it, Americans kept drinking it. And look what happened to our obesity rates: 1960, we were at 13.4%. By 2024? We hit 43% adult obesity. Coincidence? The FDA finally proposed banning BVO in 2023, but seriously — 65 years later?"

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** 
- "1958: FDA says BVO is 'safe' ✅"
- "EU & Japan: BANNED ❌"
- "2023: FDA finally acts! 😤"
- "65 YEARS LATER"

**SOUND:** Music builds tension

---

### [35-45s] CTA
**VISUAL:** Creator holds bottle away from camera with disgusted expression, then leans in close to camera with serious, direct gaze.

**VOICEOVER:** (Confident, call-to-action tone) "Read your labels, beautiful! Your health can't wait another 65 years for the FDA to catch up."

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "READ YOUR LABELS! 👀"

**SOUND:** Music drops to emphasize final message
