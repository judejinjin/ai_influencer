### PRODUCTION DETAILS

**PROPS NEEDED:**
- Kalona SuperNatural cream-top whole milk (glass bottle)
- Horizon Ultra-Pasteurized whole milk (carton)
- 2 clear drinking glasses
- Phone or printed nutrition comparison chart
- Clean kitchen towel for spills

**SHOOTING NOTES:**
- Use natural lighting near a window to show milk clarity
- Ensure cream layer is visible - let milk sit undisturbed for at least 30 minutes before filming
- Use macro lens or close-up setting for cream layer shots
- Keep background clean and minimal - white or light wood counter works best
- Film pouring shots in slow motion if possible for dramatic effect
