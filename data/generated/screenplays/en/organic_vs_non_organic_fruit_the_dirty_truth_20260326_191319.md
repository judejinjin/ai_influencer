# ORGANIC VS NON-ORGANIC FRUIT: THE DIRTY TRUTH

## 

**PROPS NEEDED:**
- 2 red apples (1 organic, 1 conventional - make sure conventional has visible wax coating)
- Clear glass water bottle
- White cutting board
- Small clear glass bowls (2)
- Paper towels
- Phone/tablet showing EWG Dirty Dozen list
- Good lighting setup for close-ups

**SHOOTING NOTES:**
- Use macro lens for extreme close-ups of water beading
- Ensure bright, clean lighting to show contrast clearly
- Film at 60fps for slow-motion water shots
- Keep background minimal and clean (white/neutral)
