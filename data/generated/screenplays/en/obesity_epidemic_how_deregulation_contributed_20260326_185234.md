### [0-2s] HOOK
**VISUAL:** Close-up of creator's face, serious expression, looking directly at camera. Quick zoom in for emphasis.
**VOICEOVER:** "Did you know US obesity rates have TRIPLED since 1960?" *(shocked, urgent tone)*
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "TRIPLED 📈"
**SOUND/MUSIC:** Sharp attention-grabbing sound effect, then upbeat but serious background music

### [2-10s] SETUP
**VISUAL:** Creator holds up two food labels - one simple, one complex. Camera pulls back to show full body, kitchen background.
**VOICEOVER:** "From 13.4% in 1960 to 43% in 2024 - that's nearly HALF of all American adults. But here's what they don't want you to know..." *(building suspense)*
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "1960: 13.4%" → "2024: 43%"
**SOUND/MUSIC:** Tension-building music

### [10-45s] CORE
**VISUAL:** [10-15s] Creator points to timeline graphic showing obesity rates climbing. [15-25s] Close-up comparison of EU vs US milk cartons. [25-35s] Creator holds up syringe prop, then points to "rBGH FREE" label. [35-45s] Split screen showing US processed food aisle vs European fresh market.

**VOICEOVER:** "Look at this timeline - obesity exploded after 1980 when food deregulation ramped up. Take rBGH, a growth hormone injected into cows to boost milk production. It's banned in the European Union, Canada, Australia, and Japan because it increases IGF-1 levels in milk - linked to cancer risk. But in America? Totally legal. Your Walmart milk could contain it right now, while Horizon Organic specifically labels 'rBGH-free' because they know consumers are concerned." *(educational, slightly outraged tone)*

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** [12s] "1980: Deregulation begins" [18s] "rBGH: BANNED in EU, Canada, Australia, Japan" [22s] "LEGAL in USA" [28s] "IGF-1 = Cancer Risk" [38s] "Horizon: rBGH-FREE" [42s] "Walmart Milk: May contain rBGH"

### [45-60s] CTA
**VISUAL:** Creator leans into camera, passionate expression. Background shows array of whole foods vs processed foods.
**VOICEOVER:** "This is just ONE additive. There are dozens more banned everywhere else but legal here. Want to see the full list? Follow for part 2, and remember - read those labels, mi amor!" *(empowering, call-to-action tone)*
