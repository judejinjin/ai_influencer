**TITLE: The Secret of Shiny Peaches 🍑**

**PROPS NEEDED:**
- 2 organic peaches (matte, natural skin)
- 2 conventional peaches (shiny, waxed)
- Sharp knife
- Small bowl of warm water
- White paper towels
- Cutting board
- Ring light or natural lighting setup

**SHOOTING NOTES:**
- Use macro lens or phone's close-up mode for texture details
- Ensure good lighting to show the contrast between matte and shiny surfaces
- Film at eye level with peaches as hero shots
- Keep background clean and minimal (white or light wood)

---

**[0-2s] HOOK**
- **VISUAL:** Extreme close-up of a super shiny conventional peach rotating slowly, light reflecting off its surface like a mirror
- **VOICEOVER:** (Intrigued tone) "Why is this peach so shiny it looks fake?"
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "🤔 TOO SHINY?"
- **SOUND:** Upbeat, mysterious intro music

**[2-10s] SETUP**
- **VISUAL:** Cut to side-by-side shot of two peaches - one shiny conventional, one matte organic. Creator's manicured hands pick up each one
- **VOICEOVER:** (Educational tone) "Let me show you what that 'perfect' shine is really hiding."
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "CONVENTIONAL vs ORGANIC"
- **SOUND:** Music continues, slightly more serious

**[10-30s] CORE PART 1**
- **VISUAL:** Creator runs the conventional peach under warm water, rubbing with paper towel. Camera shows close-up of waxy residue coming off on the towel
- **VOICEOVER:** (Revealing tone) "That shine? It's carnauba wax, shellac, or petroleum-based coatings applied after harvest. The EWG's 2023 Dirty Dozen report shows conventional peaches can contain residues from over 15 different pesticides, including organophosphates banned in Europe."
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "WAX + 15+ PESTICIDES" → "BANNED IN EUROPE"
- **SOUND:** Music builds tension

**[30-42s] CORE PART 2**
- **VISUAL:** Cut to organic peach - creator runs it under same water, towel stays clean. Side-by-side comparison of the towels
- **VOICEOVER:** (Empowering tone) "Organic peaches? No synthetic pesticides, no wax coatings. USDA Organic standards prohibit both. That natural, matte skin? That's how peaches are supposed to look."
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "USDA ORGANIC = NO WAX, NO PESTICIDES"
- **SOUND:** Music becomes uplifting

**[42-45s] CTA**
- **VISUAL:** Creator takes a bite of the organic peach, juice dripping naturally. Winks at camera
- **VOICEOVER:** (Confident, flirty tone) "Your skin deserves better than wax. So do you."
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "CHOOSE ORGANIC 🍑"
- **SOUND:** Music peaks and fades
