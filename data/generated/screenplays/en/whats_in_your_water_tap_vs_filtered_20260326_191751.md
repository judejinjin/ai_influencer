# WATER FILTER COMPARISON SCREENPLAY

## 

**TITLE:** What's in Your Water? Tap vs. Filtered!

### [0-2s] HOOK
**VISUAL:** Extreme close-up of creator's face, serious expression, holding two identical clear glasses
**VOICEOVER:** "You drink this every day..." (pause for dramatic effect)
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "TAP vs. FILTERED 🚰"
**SOUND/MUSIC:** Suspenseful buildup sound

### [2-10s] SETUP
**VISUAL:** Wide shot of kitchen counter. Creator places glasses side by side. Camera zooms in as she fills left glass from tap, right glass from Berkey gravity filter
**VOICEOVER:** "But do you know what's actually IN your water? Let's test tap versus filtered and see what you've been drinking."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** Left glass: "TAP WATER" | Right glass: "FILTERED WATER"
**SOUND/MUSIC:** Transition to educational/investigative music

### [10-45s] CORE
**VISUAL:** [10-15s] Creator holds TDS meter, dips into tap water glass. Extreme close-up of digital display showing reading
**VOICEOVER:** "This TDS meter measures dissolved solids - chemicals, minerals, and contaminants."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "TDS = Total Dissolved Solids"

**VISUAL:** [15-20s] TDS meter in tap water shows high reading (e.g., 245 ppm)
**VOICEOVER:** "Tap water: 245 parts per million. According to the EWG Tap Water Database, most US tap water contains PFAS forever chemicals, microplastics, chlorine, and pharmaceutical residues."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "TAP: 245 PPM ⚠️"

**VISUAL:** [20-25s] Creator moves TDS meter to filtered water glass, close-up of much lower reading
**VOICEOVER:** "Filtered water through my Berkey system: only 23 parts per million."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "FILTERED: 23 PPM ✅"

**VISUAL:** [25-35s] Split screen graphic showing contaminants: left side shows chlorine molecule, PFAS chemical structure, microplastic particles; right side shows clear water droplet
**VOICEOVER:** "A quality carbon block or reverse osmosis filter removes 95 to 99 percent of chlorine, lead from old pipes, PFAS chemicals, and disinfection byproducts that your municipal water treatment can't eliminate."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "95-99% CONTAMINANT REMOVAL"

**VISUAL:** [35-45s] Creator drinks from filtered water glass, satisfied expression
**VOICEOVER:** "These dissolved solids? You're drinking them every single day. Your body deserves better."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "YOUR BODY DESERVES CLEAN WATER"

### [45-60s] CTA
**VISUAL:** Creator holds both glasses up to camera, then sets down tap water glass and keeps filtered water
**VOICEOVER:** "Which water are YOU choosing? Drop a 💧 if you're ready to upgrade your hydration game!"
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "WHICH DO YOU CHOOSE? 💧"
**SOUND/MUSIC:** Upbeat, empowering outro music

---

**PROPS NEEDED:**
- Digital TDS meter (Total Dissolved Solids tester)
- 2 identical clear glasses
- Berkey water filter system (or similar gravity filter)
- Tap water source
- Small towel for cleanup

**SHOOTING NOTES:**
- Use macro lens for extreme close-ups of TDS meter display
- Ensure good lighting on water glasses to show clarity
- Shoot TDS readings multiple times to get clearest display
- Consider shooting split-screen graphics separately for post-production overlay

**HASHTAGS:** #TapWaterTruth #FilteredWater #CleanWater #HealthyHydration #WaterQuality #PFAS #HealthyLiving #WaterFilter

**MUSIC:** Educational/investigative music transitioning to upbeat empowerment track
