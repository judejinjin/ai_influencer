# BATTLE OF THE SAUCES: HOMEMADE VS. JARRED

## 

### SCREENPLAY

**[0-2s] HOOK**
- **VISUAL:** Close-up of creator's shocked face, then quick cut to a chocolate chip cookie next to a jar of Prego Traditional sauce
- **VOICEOVER:** (gasping) "You won't believe which sauce has more sugar than a cookie!"
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "SUGAR SHOCK! 🍪 vs 🍝"
- **SOUND/MUSIC:** Dramatic sting, upbeat trending sound

**[2-10s] SETUP**
- **VISUAL:** Split-screen setup - left side shows fresh ingredients (tomatoes, garlic, basil, olive oil) on marble counter, right side shows Prego jar with ingredient label facing camera
- **VOICEOVER:** (energetic) "Let's settle this once and for all - homemade versus jarred pasta sauce. The results will blow your mind!"
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "HOMEMADE" (left) | "JARRED" (right)
- **SOUND/MUSIC:** Upbeat cooking music building tension

**[10-30s] CORE - Part 1**
- **VISUAL:** Creator picks up Prego jar, points to ingredient list, then holds up Pepperidge Farm Milano cookie
- **VOICEOVER:** (incredulous) "This Prego Traditional sauce has 12 grams of sugar per cup - that's MORE than a Pepperidge Farm Milano cookie at 10 grams! Plus it's loaded with soybean oil, natural flavors, and citric acid."
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "PREGO: 12g sugar" | "MILANO COOKIE: 10g sugar"
- **SOUND/MUSIC:** Suspenseful music

**[30-45s] CORE - Part 2**
- **VISUAL:** Creator dices fresh tomatoes, crushes garlic, tears basil leaves. Quick montage of sautéing in olive oil
- **VOICEOVER:** (passionate) "Meanwhile, my homemade version? Fresh San Marzano tomatoes, organic garlic, fresh basil, and extra virgin olive oil. ZERO added sugar, ZERO preservatives, ZERO artificial anything!"
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "HOMEMADE: 0g added sugar ✨"
- **SOUND/MUSIC:** Satisfying cooking sounds, sizzling

**[45-60s] CTA**
- **VISUAL:** Side-by-side taste test - creator tastes jarred sauce (grimaces slightly), then homemade (eyes light up, chef's kiss)
- **VOICEOVER:** (confident) "Your taste buds AND your body will thank you. Which team are you on - processed or fresh? Tell me below!"
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "TEAM FRESH or TEAM PROCESSED? 👇"
- **SOUND/MUSIC:** Uplifting resolution music

### PRODUCTION DETAILS

**PROPS NEEDED:**
- Fresh San Marzano tomatoes (4-5 whole)
- Fresh garlic bulb
- Fresh basil bunch
- Extra virgin olive oil bottle
- Prego Traditional pasta sauce jar
- Pepperidge Farm Milano cookies package
- Two small bowls for tasting
- Sharp knife and cutting board
- Small sauté pan
- Measuring spoons

**SHOOTING NOTES:**
- Use ring light for even lighting on ingredient close-ups
- Split-screen effect can be achieved in post or with two phone setup
- Get macro shots of ingredient labels for clarity
- Capture steam and sizzling sounds during cooking
- Film reaction shots with good lighting on face
