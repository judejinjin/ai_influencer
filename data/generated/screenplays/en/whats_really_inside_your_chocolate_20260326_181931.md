# CHOCOLATE COMPARISON SCREENPLAY

## 

**TITLE:** What's Really Inside Your Chocolate?
**DURATION:** 45 seconds
**FORMAT:** Vertical (9:16) TikTok

### SCREENPLAY

**[0-2s] HOOK**
- **VISUAL:** Close-up shot of creator's face, looking directly into camera with raised eyebrow and slight smirk. She's holding two chocolate bars behind her back.
- **VOICEOVER:** "Surprise: Not all chocolate is created equal!" (delivered with playful, conspiratorial tone)
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "NOT ALL CHOCOLATE IS EQUAL 😱"
- **SOUND/MUSIC:** Upbeat, attention-grabbing intro beat

**[2-10s] SETUP**
- **VISUAL:** Medium shot of creator revealing both chocolate bars. Left hand holds a dark chocolate bar (Lindt Excellence 70% Dark), right hand holds a Hershey's Milk Chocolate bar. Split-screen effect dividing the frame.
- **VOICEOVER:** "Let me show you the shocking difference between real chocolate and what most Americans are eating every day."
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** Left side: "DARK CHOCOLATE" | Right side: "CANDY BAR"
- **SOUND/MUSIC:** Suspenseful transition sound

**[10-30s] CORE - PART 1**
- **VISUAL:** Extreme close-up of ingredient lists. Creator's manicured finger points to specific ingredients on each package. Quick cuts between the two labels.
- **VOICEOVER:** "This Lindt dark chocolate? Five ingredients: cocoa mass, sugar, cocoa butter, vanilla, and soy lecithin. But this Hershey's bar has over 11 ingredients including PGPR and TBHQ."
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** Ingredients highlighted in real-time as mentioned
- **SOUND/MUSIC:** Investigative, documentary-style background music

**[30-40s] CORE - PART 2**
- **VISUAL:** Creator breaks both chocolates in half, showing the texture difference. Then graphic overlay showing world map with red X's over certain countries.
- **VOICEOVER:** "TBHQ is a petroleum-derived preservative that's actually banned in Japan and restricted in the European Union. Yet it's in most American candy bars!"
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "TBHQ = PETROLEUM PRESERVATIVE" / "BANNED IN JAPAN 🚫"
- **SOUND/MUSIC:** Dramatic emphasis sound effect

**[40-45s] CTA**
- **VISUAL:** Creator takes a bite of the dark chocolate with an exaggerated satisfied expression, then points directly at camera.
- **VOICEOVER:** "Your body deserves better, mi amor. Check those labels!"
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "READ YOUR LABELS! 👀"
- **SOUND/MUSIC:** Uplifting conclusion beat

### PRODUCTION DETAILS

**PROPS NEEDED:**
- Lindt Excellence 70% Dark Chocolate bar
- Hershey's Milk Chocolate bar
- Ring light for close-up ingredient shots
- White backdrop or clean kitchen counter

**SHOOTING NOTES:**
- Use macro lens for extreme close-ups of ingredient labels
- Ensure good lighting for text readability
- Shoot chocolate break in slow motion for dramatic effect
- Keep background minimal and clean

#ChocolateTruth #ReadYourLabels #HealthyEating #FoodFacts #DarkChocolate #ProcessedFood #HealthyLifestyle #FoodEducation

Trending investigative/expose sound or dramatic reveal audio
