# SCREENPLAY: BANNED ADDITIVES EXPOSÉ



## TIME-CODED SCREENPLAY

**[0-2s] HOOK**
- **VISUAL**: Extreme close-up of creator's shocked face, eyes wide, holding two identical-looking snack packages
- **VOICEOVER**: "These additives are BANNED globally..." (dramatic pause, whispered tone)
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT**: "BANNED WORLDWIDE" in bold red letters
- **SOUND/MUSIC**: Dramatic sting, sudden silence

**[2-10s] SETUP**
- **VISUAL**: Quick montage - creator's hands placing US snack on left, EU version on right. Cut to world map animation showing red X marks across Europe, Canada, Australia
- **VOICEOVER**: "...but somehow they're still in YOUR American snacks! While 32 countries have banned these ingredients, the FDA says 'perfectly safe.'" (skeptical tone)
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT**: "32 COUNTRIES vs USA" split screen
- **SOUND/MUSIC**: Upbeat investigative music builds

**[10-25s] CORE PART 1**
- **VISUAL**: Close-up of Froot Loops box ingredients panel, creator's manicured finger pointing to specific additives
- **VOICEOVER**: "Take Kellogg's Froot Loops - the US version contains Red Dye 40, Yellow 6, and BHT preservative. The exact same cereal sold in Europe? Natural beetroot extract and turmeric." (matter-of-fact tone)
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT**: "RED DYE 40 = HYPERACTIVITY" appears over US box
- **SOUND/MUSIC**: Investigative music continues

**[25-35s] CORE PART 2**
- **VISUAL**: Split screen showing obesity statistics rising on animated graph, creator pointing to timeline
- **VOICEOVER**: "Here's what's wild - US obesity rates jumped from 15% in 1980 to 43% in 2024. That's the same period when the FDA started allowing more food additives while other countries banned them." (concerned tone)
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT**: "1980: 15%" morphs to "2024: 43%"
- **SOUND/MUSIC**: Music becomes more serious

**[35-45s] CORE PART 3**
- **VISUAL**: Creator holding Mountain Dew bottle, then European equivalent, dramatic comparison
- **VOICEOVER**: "Mountain Dew contains brominated vegetable oil - banned in the EU since 2022 because it builds up in your tissues. But Pepsi still sells it here with zero warnings." (frustrated tone)
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT**: "BUILDS UP IN TISSUES" with warning symbol
- **SOUND/MUSIC**: Music reaches climax

**[45-50s] CTA**
- **VISUAL**: Creator looking directly at camera, passionate expression, holding both products
- **VOICEOVER**: "Start reading those labels, gorgeous! Your body deserves better than being a chemistry experiment." (empowering tone)
- **ON-SCREEN TEXT**: "READ YOUR LABELS"
- **SOUND/MUSIC**: Uplifting resolution
