# SCREENPLAY: WHY THE US LOVES YELLOW 5 & 6

## 

**TITLE:** Why the US Loves Yellow 5 & 6!
**DURATION:** 45 seconds
**VERTICAL FORMAT:** 9:16 TikTok/Instagram Reels

### [0-2s] HOOK
**VISUAL:** Extreme close-up of creator's glossy lips as she bites into a bright yellow Skittles candy. Camera pulls back to reveal her shocked expression, eyes wide.
**VOICEOVER:** (Gasps dramatically) "Did you know?" (pause for effect)
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "WAIT... WHAT?! 😱"
**SOUND:** Dramatic gasp sound effect, trending suspenseful audio begins

### [2-10s] SETUP  
**VISUAL:** Quick montage: Creator's hands holding various yellow foods - Kraft Mac & Cheese box, Mountain Dew bottle, Lucky Charms cereal, Doritos Nacho Cheese bag. Each item fills the frame for 1-2 seconds.
**VOICEOVER:** "These bright yellow foods we love? They're packed with Yellow 5 and Yellow 6 - artificial dyes that are literally banned or restricted in other countries!"
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "YELLOW 5 & 6" with arrow pointing to products
**SOUND:** Upbeat, educational music continues

### [10-30s] CORE PART 1
**VISUAL:** Split screen - left side shows the same products, right side shows a printed world map with Norway, Finland, and Austria highlighted in red. Creator points to each country.
**VOICEOVER:** "Norway completely bans Yellow 5, also called Tartrazine. Finland restricts it heavily. Austria? Same deal. But here's the kicker - in the European Union, any food with these dyes must carry a warning label."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** Left: "BANNED/RESTRICTED" Right: "USA: TOTALLY FINE! 🤷‍♀️"
**SOUND:** Serious, investigative music

### [30-42s] CORE PART 2
**VISUAL:** Close-up of creator holding a European candy package showing the actual EU warning label. Cut to her reading it directly to camera.
**VOICEOVER:** "The EU warning literally says 'may have adverse effect on activity and attention in children.' Translation? These dyes can make kids hyperactive. Yet American obesity rates have skyrocketed from 13.4% in 1960 to 43% in 2024."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "EU WARNING LABEL" / "1960: 13.4%" → "2024: 43%"
**SOUND:** Concerned, informative tone in music

### [42-45s] CTA
**VISUAL:** Creator looks directly at camera with raised eyebrows, holding up both the American and European versions of similar products.
**VOICEOVER:** "So... why are we the guinea pigs?"
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "FOLLOW FOR MORE FOOD TRUTHS 👀"
**SOUND:** Music cuts to silence for impact
