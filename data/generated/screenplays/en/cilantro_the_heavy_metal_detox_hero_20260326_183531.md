**PROPS NEEDED:**
- Fresh cilantro bunches (3-4 for multiple takes)
- Sharp chef's knife
- Wooden cutting board
- Colorful prepared dish (bowl with quinoa, vegetables, or salad)
- Small bowl of water for washing

**SHOOTING NOTES:**
- Use natural window light supplemented with ring light for close-ups
- Overhead shots require tripod with adjustable arm
- Keep cilantro in water between takes to maintain freshness
- Shoot washing scene near sink with good lighting
- Have backup cilantro for multiple takes of chopping sequences
