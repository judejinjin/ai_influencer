# SCREENPLAY: THE MILK ADDITIVE BANNED EVERYWHERE BUT THE US

## 

**TITLE:** "The Milk Additive Banned Everywhere But the US"

### [0-2s] HOOK
**VISUAL:** Extreme close-up of creator's face, looking shocked/concerned directly at camera. Quick zoom into her eyes.
**VOICEOVER:** (Whispered, dramatic) "Why is this milk additive banned worldwide?"
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "BANNED WORLDWIDE? 🚫"
**SOUND:** Sharp, attention-grabbing sound effect

### [2-10s] SETUP
**VISUAL:** Creator holds up two milk cartons - one labeled "USA" and another with "EU/CANADA/AUSTRALIA" labels. Split-screen effect showing the contrast.
**VOICEOVER:** (Conversational but serious) "Ladies, we need to talk about what's hiding in American milk that the rest of the world said 'hell no' to."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "USA vs THE WORLD 🥛"
**SOUND:** Subtle background music begins - mysterious/investigative tone

### [10-25s] CORE PART 1
**VISUAL:** Close-up of milk carton ingredient label, finger pointing to "rBST" text. Camera zooms in on the small print.
**VOICEOVER:** (Educational, slightly outraged) "It's called rBGH or rBST - Recombinant Bovine Growth Hormone. This synthetic hormone is injected into cows to make them produce up to 15% more milk."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "rBGH/rBST = More Milk = More Money 💰"
**SOUND:** Investigative music continues

### [25-40s] CORE PART 2
**VISUAL:** Animated world map showing countries in red (banned) vs green (allowed). Creator gestures to each region.
**VOICEOVER:** (Listing with emphasis) "Banned in the European Union since 1999, banned in Canada since 1999, Australia, Japan, and Israel all said no. But here in the US? Still perfectly legal."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "BANNED: EU, Canada, Australia, Japan, Israel ❌"
**SOUND:** Music builds tension

### [40-50s] CORE PART 3
**VISUAL:** Creator looking directly at camera, holding up a glass of milk, then setting it down with concern.
**VOICEOVER:** (Serious, health-focused) "The FDA approved it in 1993, but studies show rBST increases IGF-1 levels in milk - a hormone linked to increased cancer risk according to multiple peer-reviewed studies."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "IGF-1 = Cancer Risk ⚠️"
**SOUND:** Music becomes more urgent

### [50-60s] CTA
**VISUAL:** Creator pointing at camera, then showing organic milk carton with "rBST-free" label.
**VOICEOVER:** (Empowering, call-to-action tone) "Look for 'rBST-free' or organic labels, mi amor. Your body deserves better than being a corporate profit experiment."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "LOOK FOR: rBST-FREE 💪"
**SOUND:** Uplifting resolution music
