**VISUAL:** Extreme close-up of creator's face, eyes wide with excitement, holding a garlic bulb near her cheek
**VOICEOVER:** "Want to unlock garlic's superpowers?" (whispered conspiratorially)

### [2-10s] SETUP
**VISUAL:** Medium shot of creator at kitchen counter, garlic bulb in one hand, knife in the other. Camera slowly zooms in on the garlic.
**VOICEOVER:** "Most people cook garlic immediately after chopping it. But science says we're doing it ALL wrong." (dramatic pause, shaking head)
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "YOU'VE BEEN DOING IT WRONG 😱"

### [10-25s] CORE PART 1
**VISUAL:** Overhead shot of cutting board. Creator's manicured hands peel garlic clove, then crush it with the flat side of a knife. Slow-motion capture of the crushing action.
**VOICEOVER:** "Here's the secret: when you crush garlic, it releases an enzyme called alliinase. This enzyme converts alliin into allicin—garlic's most powerful compound with antibacterial and antiviral properties."
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "ALLIIN → ALLICIN" with arrow animation
**SOUND/MUSIC:** Satisfying crushing sound, upbeat educational music

### [25-35s] CORE PART 2
**VISUAL:** Close-up of crushed garlic on cutting board. Creator sets phone timer to 10 minutes. Split screen showing timer counting down (sped up) and garlic sitting.
**VOICEOVER:** "But here's the game-changer: let that crushed garlic sit for exactly 10 minutes. This waiting period maximizes allicin formation by up to 300%!"
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "WAIT 10 MINUTES" "300% MORE ALLICIN!"
**SOUND/MUSIC:** Timer ticking sound, triumphant music sting

### [35-43s] CORE PART 3
**VISUAL:** Quick montage of creator adding the rested garlic to a sizzling pan, then to a salad, then making garlic oil. All shots are dynamic and appetizing.
**VOICEOVER:** "Now you can cook with it, eat it raw, or make garlic oil. You're getting maximum health benefits every time!"
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "MAXIMUM BENEFITS UNLOCKED ✅"
**SOUND/MUSIC:** Upbeat cooking montage music

### [43-45s] CTA
**VISUAL:** Creator back to camera, holding up a garlic bulb with a wink
**VOICEOVER:** "Try this tonight and let me know—did you feel the garlic power?"
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "TRY TONIGHT! COMMENT BELOW 👇"
**SOUND/MUSIC:** Playful outro music

## PRODUCTION DETAILS

**PROPS NEEDED:**
- Fresh garlic bulbs (3-4 bulbs for multiple takes)
- Sharp chef's knife
- Cutting board (preferably wood or bamboo for aesthetic)
- Garlic crusher/press
- Phone or kitchen timer
- Small pan for cooking demonstration
- Olive oil
- Small bowl for salad demo

**SHOOTING NOTES:**
- Use macro lens for extreme close-ups of garlic crushing
- Ensure good overhead lighting for cutting board shots
- Capture crushing action in both real-time and slow-motion
- Use ring light for face shots to enhance eye sparkle
- Consider using a tripod with overhead arm for consistent top-down shots
