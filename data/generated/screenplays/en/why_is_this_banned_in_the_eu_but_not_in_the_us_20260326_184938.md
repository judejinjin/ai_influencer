# FULL SCREENPLAY: BANNED CANDY INGREDIENTS



**TITLE:** "Why is this banned in the EU but not in the US?"

## SCREENPLAY

### [0-2s] HOOK
**VISUAL:** Extreme close-up of creator's shocked face, eyes wide, holding a colorful piece of candy (Skittles) inches from camera
**VOICEOVER:** "Did you know your candy might have banned ingredients?" (urgent, conspiratorial whisper)
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "BANNED INGREDIENTS? 🚫"
**SOUND:** Dramatic sting, then upbeat trending TikTok sound begins

### [2-10s] SETUP
**VISUAL:** Quick montage: Creator's hands laying out various colorful candies (Skittles, M&Ms, Mentos, Trident gum) on white marble counter. Cut to close-up of ingredient labels being flipped and examined
**VOICEOVER:** "This little white powder called Titanium Dioxide is in SO many of your favorite treats. But here's the tea..." (building suspense)
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "Titanium Dioxide = E171"
**SOUND:** Paper rustling, candy clattering on counter

### [10-35s] CORE CONTENT
**VISUAL:** Split screen showing EU flag vs US flag. Creator holds up Skittles package, points to ingredients. Cut to dramatic zoom on "Titanium Dioxide" in ingredient list. Animation overlay of DNA strand breaking apart
**VOICEOVER:** "The European Food Safety Authority banned this stuff in 2022 because their assessment showed it can literally damage your DNA - that's called genotoxicity, babe. But in America? We're still munching on it in Skittles, M&Ms, Mentos, Trident gum, and even coffee creamer!" (matter-of-fact but concerned tone)
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "EU: BANNED 2022 🇪🇺" / "USA: STILL LEGAL 🇺🇸" / "EFSA: DNA DAMAGE RISK"
**SOUND:** Whoosh sound effects for screen transitions

### [35-45s] SHOCKING STAT
**VISUAL:** Creator looks directly at camera, gesturing with hands. Quick flash of obesity statistics as animated numbers
**VOICEOVER:** "And get this - US obesity rates have tripled since 1960. We went from 13% of adults in 1960 to 43% in 2024. Coincidence? I think not." (raising eyebrow, skeptical expression)
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "1960: 13% obese" / "2024: 43% obese" / "TRIPLED! 📈"

### [45-50s] CTA
**VISUAL:** Creator holding up phone showing ingredient scanner app, then tossing candy in trash dramatically
**VOICEOVER:** "Start reading those labels, gorgeous. Your DNA will thank you later." (wink and finger guns)
**ON-SCREEN TEXT:** "READ YOUR LABELS! 💅"
**SOUND:** Phone notification sound, trash can lid slam
